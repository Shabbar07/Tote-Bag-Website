Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options and delivery times?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) for ₹50 and express shipping (2-3 business days) for ₹150. Free shipping is available on orders over ₹999.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused, in original condition, and with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders (20+ items) and wholesale partnerships. Contact us at sales@totestore.com for more information.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order in your account dashboard or contact our support team.
